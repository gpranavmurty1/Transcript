**US-11: Milestone Progress Persistence**
> As a new hire, I want my milestone completion status to be saved automatically so that I don't lose my progress if I close the browser.

**1. Business Context**
Since the onboarding journey spans two weeks, it is guaranteed users will have multiple distinct sessions in the application. Seamlessly saving and loading progress ensures trust in the tool and removes the friction of re-entering state.

**2. In Scope**
- Saving the toggled state of a milestone to the backend (Firebase Firestore) immediately when changed.
- Fetching and populating the user's completed milestones upon login/refresh.

**3. Out of Scope**
- Offline mode or local storage fallback (application assumes standard continuous internet connectivity while on the corporate network).
- Detailed audit logging of exactly *when* a milestone was checked (only storing boolean state).

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* Secure user authentication (Epic 1) is functioning, providing a unique User ID.
- *Dependency:* Firebase Firestore database is provisioned and linked to the frontend.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Data point saving*
**Given** I have an active internet connection and am logged in
**When** I mark a milestone as complete or incomplete
**Then** the new state must be immediately synced to my user document in Firebase Firestore without requiring a manual "Save" button.

*Scenario 2: Session persistence*
**Given** I have completed 4 milestones during a session
**When** I close the browser tab, wait, and log back into the Smart Onboarding Companion
**Then** my Journey view must accurately load and reflect the exact 4 milestones as completed, preserving my progress.
