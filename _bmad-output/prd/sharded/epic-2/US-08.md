**US-08: Role-Specific 1-on-1 Milestones — Designers**
> As a new Designer, I want guided prompts to conduct 1-on-1s within my first 2 weeks so that I build relationships with key stakeholders.

**1. Business Context**
Designers must quickly establish critique loops and understand the broader product vision. Prompting them to meet with their immediate product triad/peers and design leadership early establishes psychological safety and accelerates design delivery within the first sprint.

**2. In Scope**
- Defining specific 1-on-1 milestones for Designers:
  - Every PM and Designer on the immediate team (focus: working styles).
  - Crew Leads (one in India, one in Australia).
  - Head of Design Practice (intro chat).
- Deep-linking these milestones to the Team Directory.

**3. Out of Scope**
- Validating the completion of these meetings via external integrations (e.g., Zoom or Google Calendar APIs).
- Mandating 1-on-1s with Engineers (assumed to happen naturally during agile ceremonies).

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* The Team Directory feature (Epic 4) must be populated with accurate contacts for Crew Leads and the Head of Design Practice.
- *Assumption:* Mentors will also help facilitate these introductions if needed.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Viewing Designer-specific Milestones*
**Given** I am a logged-in new hire who selected "Design" as my role
**When** I view my "Week 1-2: Role-Specific Training" section
**Then** I must see the three designated 1-on-1 milestones (Immediate Team, Crew Leads, Head of Design Practice).

*Scenario 2: Role Exclusivity*
**Given** I am a new hire assigned to the Engineering or Product roles
**When** I view my onboarding journey
**Then** I must NOT see the Designer-specific 1-on-1 milestones (e.g., meeting the Head of Design Practice).
