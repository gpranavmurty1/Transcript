**US-07: Role-Specific 1-on-1 Milestones — Product Managers**
> As a new Product Manager, I want guided prompts to conduct 1-on-1s within my first 2 weeks so that I build relationships with key stakeholders.

**1. Business Context**
For a Product Manager, influencing without authority and understanding team dynamics is critical to early success. Structuring introductory 1-on-1s ensures PMs build their foundational network rapidly, aligning with our goal to reduce time-to-productivity to 2 weeks.

**2. In Scope**
- Defining specific 1-on-1 milestones for Product Managers:
  - Every PM and Designer on the immediate team (focus: working styles).
  - Crew Leads (one in India, one in Australia).
  - Head of Product Practice (intro chat).
- Deep-linking these milestones to the Team Directory for easy contact discovery.

**3. Out of Scope**
- Automated calendar scheduling (the app only prompts the user to reach out; it does not integrate with Google Calendar APIs).
- Tracking whether the 1-on-1 actually occurred (relies on user self-reporting via the milestone checkbox).

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* The Team Directory feature (Epic 4) must be populated with accurate contacts for Crew Leads and the Head of Product Practice.
- *Assumption:* The user understands how to use Slack or email to schedule these meetings once directed to the right person.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Viewing PM-specific Milestones*
**Given** I am a logged-in new hire who selected "Product Management" as my role
**When** I view my "Week 1-2: Role-Specific Training" section
**Then** I must see the three designated 1-on-1 milestones (Immediate Team, Crew Leads, Head of Product Practice)
**And** these milestones must not appear for users in other roles.

*Scenario 2: Finding Contacts through the Milestone*
**Given** I am viewing one of the PM 1-on-1 milestones
**When** I click on the provided action link or contact reference
**Then** I am routed to the "Team Directory" where the relevant individuals (e.g., Crew Leads) are visible.
