**US-06: Tool Familiarity Milestone (All Roles)**
> As a new hire, I want to be guided to set up and explore Miro, Figma, and AntiGravity within my first two weeks so that I'm prepared for day-to-day work.

**1. Business Context**
To ensure all team members (Product, Design, Engineering) can collaborate efficiently, an early understanding of our core tool stack is crucial. Familiarity with Miro (for whiteboarding), Figma (for design/wireframing), and AntiGravity (our internal AI tool) ensures cross-functional communication is seamless.

**2. In Scope**
- Providing distinct milestones for exploring Miro, Figma, and AntiGravity.
- Delivering these milestones to all roles by the end of Week 2.
- Linking each milestone to the corresponding "getting started" Notion guide.

**3. Out of Scope**
- Role-specific advanced tool training (e.g., teaching an Engineer how to build a Figma plugin, or a Designer how to use AntiGravity APIs).
- Tracking the actual usage metrics of these tools within the first two weeks.

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Assumption:* The Notion resources for Miro, Figma, and AntiGravity onboarding are already written, up-to-date, and accessible to new hires.
- *Dependency:* The user has access provisioned for these tools by IT prior to this milestone.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Viewing tool familiarity milestones*
**Given** I am a new hire of any role (Product, Design, or Engineering)
**When** I view my milestones for "Week 1-2: Core Systems"
**Then** I must see specific milestones dedicated to achieving basic familiarity with Miro, Figma, and AntiGravity
**And** these milestones must be due by the end of Week 2.

*Scenario 2: Accessing tool resources*
**Given** I am viewing a tool familiarity milestone (e.g., for Figma)
**When** I want to learn more or complete the milestone
**Then** the milestone card must contain a direct hyperlink to the relevant Notion onboarding resource for that specific tool.
