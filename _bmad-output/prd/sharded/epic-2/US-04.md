**US-04: View My Onboarding Journey**
> As a new hire, I want to see a clear day-by-day/week-by-week onboarding plan so that I know exactly what to do and when.

**1. Business Context**
The onboarding journey is the central feature of the Smart Onboarding Companion. By providing a clear, structured, and timeframe-bound plan, we can significantly reduce the "time-to-productivity" from the baseline of 5 weeks down to the target of 2 weeks. This also prevents new hires from feeling lost or overwhelmed by ad-hoc Slack messages.

**2. In Scope**
- Displaying a complete list of onboarding milestones.
- Grouping milestones logically by timeframe and mandatory/role-specific status.
- Showing detailed milestone cards (title, description, timeframe, estimated time, and status).
- Displaying a visual progress bar of completion.
- Highlighting overdue milestones based on start date.

**3. Out of Scope**
- Editing or creating new milestones from this view (Admin MVP out of scope).
- Tracking time actually spent on a milestone (only estimates are shown).
- Dynamic restructuring of the journey based on completion speed.

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* User must have completed the Role Selection and Skills Assessment steps.
- *Dependency:* The application must know the user's start date to calculate "overdue" milestones.
- *Assumption:* The pre-defined milestone configuration is available in Firestore or a local configuration file.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Viewing the Journey layout*
**Given** I am a logged-in new hire who has selected my role
**When** I navigate to the "My Journey" section from the main sidebar
**Then** I should see my milestones grouped into distinct collapsible sections: "Week 1: Foundations", "Week 1-2: Core Systems", and "Week 1-2: Role-Specific Training"
**And** I should see a progress bar at the top reflecting the percentage of completed milestones out of the total required for my role.

*Scenario 2: Viewing a Milestone Card*
**Given** I am viewing the "My Journey" page
**When** I look at a specific milestone card
**Then** it must display a clear title, a brief description/instruction, a due timeframe, the estimated time to complete, and a completion status indicator.

*Scenario 3: Overdue Milestones*
**Given** I have a milestone with a due timeframe that has passed relative to my start date
**When** I view the "My Journey" page
**Then** that specific milestone card should be visually highlighted (e.g., with a red border and an "Overdue" tag).
