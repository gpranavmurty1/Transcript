**US-10: Mark Milestone as Complete**
> As a new hire, I want to mark milestones as complete so that I can track my progress.

**1. Business Context**
A core value proposition of the Smart Onboarding Companion is giving new hires a sense of progression and clarity. Allowing users to explicitly mark tasks as "done" provides psychological reward and helps them easily identify what remains to be completed.

**2. In Scope**
- Interactive UI element (checkbox or "Mark Complete" button) on every milestone card.
- Visual state change distinguishing completed milestones from pending ones.
- Immediate recalculation of the journey's overall completion percentage.

**3. Out of Scope**
- Complex gamification, animations (e.g., confetti), or earnable badges upon completion (MVP styling only).
- Manager approval or verification (self-attestation only).

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* The milestone cards (from US-04) are fully rendered and accessible in the DOM.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Marking a task complete*
**Given** I am viewing an incomplete milestone card
**When** I click the "Mark Complete" checkbox or button
**Then** the milestone should visually change to a "completed" state (e.g., greyed out text, checked icon, or subdued background)
**And** the overall journey progress bar should increase proportionately.

*Scenario 2: Unmarking a task*
**Given** I have a previously completed milestone
**When** I uncheck or click "Mark Incomplete"
**Then** the milestone's visual state reverts to standard/active pending styling
**And** the overall journey progress bar decreases proportionately.
