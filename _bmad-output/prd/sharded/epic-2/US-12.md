**US-12: Week 2 Reflection Milestone (All Roles)**
> As a new hire, I want a prompted reflection at the end of Week 2 so that I can provide feedback on my onboarding experience.

**1. Business Context**
The Smart Onboarding Companion aims to lower time-to-productivity, but qualitative feedback is necessary to continually refine the process. By baking a reflection prompt directly into the final week's milestones, we capture feedback while the experience is fresh in the new hire's mind.

**2. In Scope**
- Displaying a dedicated milestone at the end of "Week 2" for all users.
- Hyperlinking to an external survey form (e.g., Google Forms).

**3. Out of Scope**
- Building a custom survey interface or feedback collection database within the Companion app itself.
- Triggering email reminders if the survey isn't filled out.

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* People Operations/HR has created and provided the URL for the external 3-question Google Form.
- *Assumption:* The link is generic (not user-specific) and the form collects the user's email if necessary.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Viewing the reflection prompt*
**Given** I am a logged-in user of any role
**When** I scroll to the final section of my Journey view (End of Week 2)
**Then** I must see a milestone titled "Week 2 Reflection" or similar
**And** it must possess a clear call-to-action button or link pointing to the feedback survey.

*Scenario 2: Accessing the survey*
**Given** I am viewing the Week 2 Reflection milestone
**When** I click the survey link
**Then** I am navigated in a new browser tab to the configured Google Form/feedback URL.
