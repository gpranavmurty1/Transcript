**US-09: Role-Specific 1-on-1 Milestones — Engineers**
> As a new Engineer, I want guided prompts to conduct 1-on-1s within my first 2 weeks so that I build relationships with key stakeholders.

**1. Business Context**
Engineers need to quickly understand the technical operations, architectural vision, and crew dynamics. While immediate team bonding happens in daily standups and pairing sessions, establishing a direct connection with Crew Leads and the Practice Head provides crucial organizational context that accelerates productivity.

**2. In Scope**
- Defining specific 1-on-1 milestones for Engineers:
  - Crew Leads (one in India, one in Australia).
  - Head of Engineering Practice (intro chat).
- Deep-linking these milestones to the Team Directory.

**3. Out of Scope**
- 1-on-1 milestones for the immediate team (explicitly excluded as engineers bond through pairing and code reviews).
- Automated meeting scheduling.

**4. Assumptions/Dependencies**
- *Dependency:* The Team Directory feature (Epic 4) must be populated with accurate contacts for Crew Leads and the Head of Engineering Practice.
- *Assumption:* Engineering managers will back up this requirement during their own check-ins.

**5. Acceptance Criteria - Given When Then**

*Scenario 1: Viewing Engineer-specific Milestones*
**Given** I am a logged-in new hire who selected "Engineering" as my role
**When** I view my "Week 1-2: Role-Specific Training" section
**Then** I must see the two designated 1-on-1 milestones (Crew Leads, Head of Engineering Practice)
**And** I must NOT see milestones requiring 1-on-1s with immediate PMs/Designers (as required by other roles).

*Scenario 2: Routing to Directory*
**Given** I am viewing an Engineer 1-on-1 milestone
**When** I click the embedded link to find my Crew Leads
**Then** I am navigated to the Team Directory filtered or focused on leadership/mentors.
